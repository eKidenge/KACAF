Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about contacting and engaging with KACAF

We pride ourselves on our 24-hour response policy. All inquiries submitted through this form are acknowledged immediately, and a detailed response from the relevant department follows within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our main line at +254 796 761 841.

Yes! Davis Masibo, our Lead Environmental Scientist, is available for technical consultations, research collaborations, and partnership discussions. You can reach him directly at +254 796 761 841 or davis.masibo@kacaf.org. For general inquiries, our team will ensure your message reaches the right person.

Becoming a member is easy and free! You can:
  • Fill out the membership category in the contact form above
  • Visit our Join KACAF page
  • Call our membership desk at +254 796 761 841
  • Visit our Kapenguria office during business hours
Membership includes access to training, networking events, and our quarterly newsletter.

KACAF welcomes partnerships with:
  • Research institutions: Collaborate on environmental studies and innovations
  • Corporations: CSR initiatives and sustainability programs
  • NGOs: Joint projects and community outreach
  • Government agencies: Policy development and implementation
  • International organizations: Funding and technical support
Contact Davis Masibo directly for partnership discussions.

You can support our work through:
  • One-time donations: Via M-Pesa Paybill or bank transfer
  • Monthly giving: Become a regular supporter
  • Sponsor a tree: Fund tree planting initiatives
  • Sponsor a farmer: Support training and resources
  • Legacy giving: Include KACAF in your will
Select "Donation & Support" in the contact form for more information.
