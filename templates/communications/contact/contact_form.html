Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries

How can I become a member?

You can join KACAF by filling out our membership application form online or visiting our office in Kapenguria. Membership is open to all individuals and organizations committed to environmental conservation.

Do you accept volunteers?

Yes! We welcome volunteers who are passionate about agroforestry and climate action. Please contact Davis Masibo directly for current volunteer opportunities.

How can I support your work?

You can support KACAF through donations, sponsoring tree planting events, or partnering with us on specific projects. Every contribution helps us grow our impact.

Do you offer training programs?

Yes, we regularly conduct training on sustainable agriculture, tree nursery management, and climate-smart practices. Check our Events page for upcoming workshops.
